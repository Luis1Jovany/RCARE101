NBRC Thinking Model

Three layers of clinical mastery

This course follows the 2027 NBRC thinking framework. All activities are designed to push you toward Layer 3.

Layer 1 — Know It
Recall normal values, formulas, and definitions. The baseline every RT must have.
Layer 2 — Apply It
Use those values in a specific patient situation. Calculate VE, VA, P/F ratio, CaO₂.
Layer 3 — Decide With It
Determine if you should recommend, monitor, change, or escalate care. This is the hallmark of the RRT.
